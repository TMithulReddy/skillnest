💎 The Credit System

No money. Just skills.

SkillNest runs entirely on a virtual credit system ensuring fair value exchange. Share your knowledge to earn credits, and spend them to learn from others.

Earn Credits

  • Teach a session → +2000 credits
  • Complete your profile → +1250 credits
  • Signup welcome bonus → +4000 credits
  • Get a 5-star rating → +500 credits
  • 7-day login streak → +750 credits

Spend Credits

  • Request a session → 1250 credits
  • Credits held in escrow until session completes
  • Full refund if teacher cancels
  • Teacher still earns credits if learner no-shows
